Section 5: Ontario Rebate Programs (Use These Exactly)
These are the real, current Ontario and federal rebate programs as of early 2026. Use these
exact program names, dollar amounts, and URLs in mock-rebate-programs.json and on
Screen 6. Do not invent program names or amounts.
Federal Programs
Canada Greener Homes Grant
- Administering body: Natural Resources Canada (NRCan)
- Maximum amount: $5,000 total per household
- Eligible upgrades: Air-source heat pumps (up to $5,000), insulation (up to $600 per
zone, up to $5,000 total), windows and doors, solar PV
- Key condition: Requires a pre- and post-retrofit EnerGuide assessment by an
NRCan-registered energy advisor. For the POC demo, display the rebate amount and
note the assessment requirement; do not simulate the assessment process.
- Application URL:
https://www.nrcan.gc.ca/energy-efficiency/homes/canada-greener-homes-grant/23230
Canada Greener Homes Loan
- Administering body: NRCan
- Maximum amount: $40,000 interest-free loan, up to 10-year term
- Eligible upgrades: Same as the Grant
- Key condition: Must complete a pre-retrofit EnerGuide assessment
- Note: This is a loan, not a grant. Display separately on Screen 6 to avoid confusion with
the grant amount.
- Application URL:
https://www.nrcan.gc.ca/energy-efficiency/homes/canada-greener-homes-loan/24286
Ontario / Utility Programs
Enbridge Home Efficiency Rebate Plus (HER+)
- Administering body: Enbridge Gas (Ontario)
- Maximum amount: Up to $10,000 for eligible retrofits
- Eligible upgrades: Air-source heat pumps (up to $6,500), insulation, air sealing, smart
thermostats, heat pump water heaters
- Key condition: Must be an Enbridge Gas customer; pre-retrofit home assessment
required
- Stackable with: Canada Greener Homes Grant (programs are designed to be stacked)
- Application URL: https://www.enbridgegas.com/home-efficiency-rebate
IESO saveONenergy Programs
- Administering body: IESO / local electric utility
- Programs include: Home Assistance Program (low-income focus), Retrofit Program,
Peak Saver Plus
- Amounts: Typical residential rebates range from $50 to $500 for appliances and
thermostats; larger amounts for eligible insulation and HVAC work
- Key condition: Must be a customer of a participating Ontario electric utility
- Application URL: https://www.ieso.ca/consumer-partnership/energy-efficiency-programs
Toronto Hydro Energy Conservation Programs
- Administering body: Toronto Hydro (Toronto customers only)
- Programs: peaksaver PLUS (smart thermostat rebate), Home Energy Savings Program
- Amounts: Smart thermostat rebate up to $75; additional conservation program rebates
vary
- Key condition: Toronto Hydro customer
- Display rule: Show this program only when the entered address is in the City of Toronto.
For all other Ontario addresses, display the generic IESO programs instead.
- Application URL: https://www.torontohydro.com/for-home/energy-conservation
Ontario Renovates Program
- Administering body: Ontario Ministry of Municipal Affairs and Housing
- Maximum amount: Forgivable loans up to $25,000 for eligible homeowners
- Key condition: Income-qualified; priority for Indigenous, Northern, and rural homeowners
- Display note: Include this program in the summary with an eligibility note. Do not assume
eligibility for the demo profile; display it as "Check eligibility" rather than "You qualify."
- Application URL:
https://www.ontario.ca/page/get-help-with-home-repairs-and-modifications
Stacking Note for the Demo
For a typical GTA homeowner replacing a gas furnace with a cold-climate heat pump and
adding insulation:
- Canada Greener Homes Grant: up to $5,000
- HER+ (Enbridge): up to $6,500 for heat pump plus up to $2,000 for insulation
- IESO/utility programs: up to $500
- Total stacked rebates: up to $14,000
This stacked total is what the "Total available rebates" figure on Screens 6 and 8 should reflect
for a heat pump plus insulation project on a typical demo profile.
