The POC is investor-ready when every item on this checklist is true. Use this list to self-check
before the first investor walkthrough.
Functional
- All 8 screens are navigable in sequence (click through the full homeowner journey
without any broken links or missing routes)
- Address autocomplete works using the Google Places API (real Canadian address
suggestions appear as you type)
- Selecting a Toronto or GTA address loads a satellite map of that property on Screen 1
and Screen 3
- Intake answers (Screen 2) feed into the recommendation engine: a gas furnace home
and an electric baseboard home produce different recommendations on Screen 4
- Screen 5 shows at least 3 equipment options for the Heat Pump category with realistic
specs, pricing, and rebate badges
- Screen 6 shows at least 3 rebate programs with real program names, real dollar
amounts, and a running total that reflects the selected upgrades (not a hardcoded
number)
- Screen 7 shows 3 contractor profiles with realistic GTA details and a "Request Quote"
CTA that opens and closes a modal
- Screen 8 shows total cost, net cost after rebates, annual savings, and payback period all
derived from the homeowner's inputs — different intake answers produce different
numbers
Visual Quality
- All 8 screens use a consistent color palette, typography, and spacing (no visual drift
between screens)
- No placeholder text anywhere (no "Lorem ipsum", no "[INSERT PRODUCT NAME]", no
"TODO", no "[TBD]")
- No broken images or missing icons
- The design looks polished and professional at first impression, not like a prototype
- Works correctly on desktop Chrome and Safari (primary investor demo environment)
- Works correctly on a mobile browser at 390px viewport width (iPhone 15 Pro width):
layout stacks correctly, no horizontal scrolling, no clipped content
- Page transitions load in under 2 to 3 seconds per screen advance on a standard
broadband connection
Demo-ability
- Deployed on Vercel with a shareable URL (no local setup required by the investor)
- A first-time visitor with no instructions can navigate the full journey without help from the
Modovate team
- The full journey (Screen 1 to Screen 8) takes 3 to 5 minutes to complete at a normal
pace
- The "Request Quote" CTA on Screen 7 responds (opens a modal — a form that does
not need to submit to a backend)
- The "Download Report" CTA on Screen 8 responds (triggers browser print, a static PDF,
or a placeholder page — something must happen)
